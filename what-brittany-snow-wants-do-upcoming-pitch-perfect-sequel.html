What Brittany Snow Wants to Do in the Upcoming Pitch Perfect Sequel

After keeping both fans and cast members guessing, Universal Studios confirmed that a Pitch Perfect sequel is coming your way in 2015. “I just found out!” Brittany Snow told reporters yesterday at the premiere of the Lifetime Original Movie Call Me Crazy: A Five Film in Los Angeles. “I talked the producers and they were like, ‘We’ve wanted to tell you for a long time, but we couldn’t.’ I was like, ‘Well, I hate finding out through Twitter!’ But it’s cool!” And for the second go-around Snow is campaigning for an encore duet with co-star Anna Kendrick, as the two sang David Guetta's "Titanium" in the original. "Hopefully, we can do another scene where we sing together." That idea—on top of catchy harmonies and Rebel Wilson's spot-on comedic timing—would be amazing.

Lil Wayne &quot;Krazy&quot; Video (Trailer)

While we still don't have a release date, Carter V season is truly upon us. Things seemed to quiet down slightly after the release of "Believe Me", "D'usse", and "Krazy", but today, Wayne has launched right back into promo-mode, releasing a brand new Drake-featuring track called "Grindin'", and a teaser for the "Krazy" video.

If you already feel like trailers for music videos are a waste of time, this may not change your mind, but it's still a must watch for any Wayne stan. The ten-second clip shows Wayne being pulled through what appears to be a mental institution in a straight jacket, a very literal take on the title, and a throwback to some classic Eminem and Mystikal videos.

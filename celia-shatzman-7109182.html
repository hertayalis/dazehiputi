Celia Shatzman - InStyle

Title: Writer

Education: Northwestern University

Location: New York, NY

Celia Shatzman has nearly two decades of experience writing about beauty and style. She's covered everything from behind the scenes at New York Fashion Week to deep dives on skincare, fragrance, hair, and makeup, to name a few.

Highlights:

  • She has traveled to Paris to interview perfume, to Guatemala to visit farmers who grow the ingredients for skincare products, and many places in between.
  • She's an alumnus of the Medill School of Journalism.
  • Her work has appeared in New York, Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar, WWD, Conde Nast Traveler, Afar, Marie Claire, Refinery29, and NYLON, and many more.

Experience

Celia is currently a beauty and style contributor at Forbes and her work has appeared in New York, Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar, WWD, Conde Nast Traveler, Afar, Travel + Leisure, Women’s Health, Marie Claire, Refinery29 and NYLON, among others. When she’s not writing, Celia enjoys traveling, learning to play tennis, and playing with her rescue dog, Watson. Check out her site at celiashatzman.com and follow her on Instagram @celiashatzman.

Education

Celia is a graduate of the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University.

Roman Reigns Drags 2020 WWE Slammy Awards

Roman Reigns had a great end to 2020, but he spent a majority of the year at home in quarantine. The fans didn’t forget that when they voted for the 2020 Slammy Awards.

Click here for our complete coverage of the 2020 Slammy Awards.

Roman Reigns was nominated for several Slammy Awards, but he didn’t win any. One fan asked why the Tribal Chief left the program empty-handed. Roman Reigns replied and said that he doesn’t need a “participation award.”

When you’re are the highest paid/most decorated Superstar in the business. You don’t need participation awards.

The 2020 Slammy Awards was a big night for Drew McIntyre. He won best male Superstar and best overall Superstar of the year. Obviously, Roman Reigns doesn’t seem to care what accolades the Scottish Warrior pulled down at the 2020 Slammy Awards.

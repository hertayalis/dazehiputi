PartyNextDoor Has Been After Kylie Jenner for 2 Months

Not long after her breakup with Tyga, Kylie Jenner has already found herself a new suitor. She caused a stir when she was seen out with PartyNextDoor, 22, (one of Drake's hip-hop/R&B proteges) a few days after breaking up with her rapper boyfriend. But it turns out that PND—whose real name is Jahron Anthony Brathwaite—was already trying to get Jenner's attention for the past two months. 

Brathwaite "actually began courting Kylie two months ago," a source told US Weekly. At the time his relationship with Jenner, 18, remained mainly platonic because she was still dating Tyga, but that changed after they broke up for good in mid May.

Jenner and PND first met when she appeared in his "Recognize" music video in 2014, but it was reportedly Drake who really helped the two connect right after Kylie's breakup. "They were just friends, but now they're definitely together," the source added.

Leave it to Kylie to bounce back from a breakup like a champ.

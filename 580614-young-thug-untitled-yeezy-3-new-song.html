Untitled (Yeezy 3)

ICYMI, Young Thug served as one of the models for the Kanye West’s Yeezy 3 show at Madison Square Garden on Thursday afternoon, where ‘Ye debuted his new album The Life Of Pablo. And after the album was done being played, Kanye gave Thugger the aux cord to perform a never-before-heard record for the crowd that was fire, and instantly brought a ton a buzz.

Well fast forward just a couple hours later and that song has now hit the internet thanks to the LA Leakers. While it’s not the official version yet, it’s still worthy of the attention and buzz the record has quickly garnered. Take a listen and let us know what you think.

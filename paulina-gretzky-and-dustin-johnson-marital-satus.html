Wayne Gretzky's daughter Paulina Gretzky is engaged to Dustin Johnson since 2013.

A native of Los Angeles, Paulina Gretzky, has made appearances in films including Gambling (2012) and Grown Ups 2 (2013). However, she is better known as the fiancee of Dustin Johnson, a professional golf player, currently plays on the PGA Tour. 

Moreover, Gretzky is the eldest daughter of the well-known ice hockey player, Wayne Gretzky, and his actress wife, Janet Jones. However, today we are exploring more details about Paulina and Dustin's relationship. 

Are Paulina Gretzky and Dustin Johnson Married?

No, the long-term couple, Paulina, 32, and Dustin, 37, are not married but in a perfect marital relationship. 

Paulina and Dustin have been engaged for eight years as of 2021. However, they still have not tied the wedding knot and claim they currently have no plans for their weddings. 

Also, they say the couple is not in a rush to marry. “I don’t know if people know if I and Dustin are really married or not. We’re not,” she stated on a New York Post. 

Neverthless, Paulina once revealed that they wanted to get married in the fall of 2014. However, the things between them didn't work out due to their busy schedules.

Likewise, the lovebirds first met in 2009, when he came over a family dinner after playing pro-am with her mother, Janet, at Tiger's tournament at Sherwood. But right after the first encounter, the couple did not start their passionate relationship. Officially, they began dating in late 2013.

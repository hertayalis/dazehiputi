I'm Replacing My Fuzzy Slipper Collection With These 6 Cozy Pairs

I have multiple pairs of slipper-like fuzzy shoes for several occasions, and I'm not afraid to wear them out and about. I wear my Ugg Tasmans similarly to my favorite sneakers. I have a cozy pair I slip into first thing in the morning and strictly only wear them around the house when I’m getting dressed. Then, I have another slip-on style with thick rubber outsoles I wear outdoors to walk the dog or grab coffee. Some people might frown upon wearing slippers outside, but these aren’t your grandma’s bunny slippers. 

There are styles meant for indoor wear based on design, but others are warm and durable enough to handle outside terrain. I plan on restocking my winter shoe collection with these six fur-lined shoes that are great for indoor and outdoor use — and they’re all $55 and under. 

Shop 6 Faux Fur-Lined Slippers and Shoes:

Dearfoams Fireside Sydney Shearling Fur Slippers

Dearfoams Fireside Sydney Shearling Fur Slippers. have been a game-changer since I discovered them last winter. They do a great job of keeping my feet warm and cozy when I take my pup out for our morning walks or read a book on my balcony. I love them so much that I gifted my whole family a pair last Christmas, and now they’re all Dearfoam stans. 

The shearling-lined slippers come in 15 colors, including brown, light blue, purple, gold, and black, and sizes 6 through 11, with some available in wide options. They feature a suede exterior and 100 percent Australian shearling interior that's moisture-wicking and temperature-regulating, so your feet stay dry and don’t overheat. They have an slip-on design for easy on and off, a memory foam cushion for added comfort, and a lightweight rubber sole that offers great grip and traction on slick and uneven surfaces. They’re great quality, hold up well outdoors, and are at half the price of competitors — which is exactly why I’m picking up another pair. 

Koolaburra by Ugg Lezly Moccasins

Growing up, moccasins were my fall and winter uniform. My mom would have to buy me a fresh pair every year because I would wear them to school nearly every day. They’re sleek enough to pass as a cozy loafer but have all the warmth and plushness of your favorite house slippers. I haven’t bought a pair in years, but I'm now considering adding one to my winter shoe rotation — particularly, this one from Ugg’s more affordable sister brand, Koolaburra. 

The classic moccasin style comes in 11 colors, including Ugg’s signature chestnut shade, red, light blue, and black, and sizes 5 through 12. They feature a silky suede exterior, wool and faux fur blend lining for warmth, and a rubber outsole for grip and traction on all surfaces.

Halluci Plush Cross-Band Slippers

While I have an extensive collection of slippers, Halluci’s Plush Cross-Band Slippers are the first pair I slip into as soon as I step out of bed in the morning. They feel like a warm hug, but don’t overheat, thanks to the criss-cross design that allows the toes to breathe. Upon initial wear, these slippers may feel a bit snug because the fur is at its fullest, but as you continue to wear them, it’ll flatten out a bit. They make for a great holiday gift (hi, mom!), but are also a cozy everyday luxury that won’t break the bank.

The slippers come in sizes S through XL and seven colors and prints, such as leopard, white, gray, pink, and dark green. There’s also a flexible, lightweight rubber outsole with a good grip and a memory foam insole for added cushioning.

Gregory Hines Net Worth: $6 Million

Gregory Hines was an American dancer, actor, singer, and choreographer born in New York City in 1949. He passed away in August 2003 at the age of 57 from liver cancer. Hines was the lead singer of rock band Severance and had a #1 hit on the Billboard R&B chart with Luther Vandross. He starred in his own TV show, The Gregory Hines Show, and had recurring roles in Will & Grace and Lost at Home. He also provided the voice of Big Bill on Little Bill. In film, Hines appeared in History of the World: Part I, Tap, and The Preacher’s Wife. He won a Daytime Emmy Award in 2003 and was nominated for multiple other awards.

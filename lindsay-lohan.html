What is Lindsay Lohans Net Worth: Biography, Net Worth &amp; more

Lindsay Lohan is a $2 million net-worth American actress, singer, songwriter, businesswoman, and producer. It’s tough not to shake one’s head when one considers Lindsay Lohan’s career and life. She was once considered the epitome of cool.

She cast herself in fantastic parts, displayed an easy humorous sensibility with a hint of sexiness, and came across as the popular girl who was still pleasant enough to hang out with the geeks.

Who is Lindsay Lohan?

Lindsay Lohan was born on July 2, 1986, in the Bronx, a borough of New York City, and grew up on Long Island, New York, in Merrick and Cold Spring Harbour. She is Dina (Sullivan) and Michael Lohan’s eldest child. Her father, a former Wall Street trader, has had multiple run-ins with the law, while her mother is a former singer and dancer.

Lohan has three younger siblings, all of whom have worked as models or actors: Michael Jr., who co-starred in The Parent Trap with Lohan, Aliana, known as “Ali,” and Dakota “Cody” Lohan. Lohan was reared as a Roman Catholic and is of Irish and Italian ancestry.

Her maternal ancestors were “well-known Irish Catholic stalwarts,” and her great-grandfather, John L. Sullivan, was a co-founder of Long Island’s Pro-life Party. In grade 11, she began homeschooling. Lohan has a natural redhead hair color.

Lindsay Lohan’s parents married in 1985, divorced when she was three, and eventually reconciled. They divorced in 2007 after a second separation in 2005.

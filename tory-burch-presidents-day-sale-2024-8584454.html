Small Kira Chevron Flap Shoulder Bag

If you’ve been in the market for a classic shoulder bag, then look no further than this Tory Burch Small Kira Chevron Flap Shoulder Bag. It comes in black, tan, gray, and white and features a front flap magnetic closure, a longer detachable crossbody strap with gold-plated chained detailing, and a shorter shoulder strap. The bag is made from a soft leather and has an internal microfiber lining. There’s one slip pocket on the inside, and the dimensions are large enough to hold the essentials like your cellphones, a cardholder, lip gloss, and keys. It also includes a dust bag to protect the purse while not in use.

Classic Rain Loafer

My go-to shoe will always be a pair of loafers, and this Tory Burch Classic Rain Loafer has officially been added to my wishlist. The style comes in three colors, including black, yellow, and white, and sizes that range from 5 through 11. The loafers have a classic silhouette and are made with a waterproof rubber exterior and a sole with a heavy jersey lining. The sole has a short heel for added arch support, features the brand’s emblem on the top of the shoe, and comes with a dust bag for safe storing.

Satin Down Vest

A cozy sweater with Tory Burch’s Satin Down Vest layered over top is the combination I didn’t know I needed — and it’s $101 off. The puffer vest comes in ivory and sizes S through XL. It features a tall funnel neck that turns into a convertible packable hood, a sleeveless design, a two-way zipper for easy on and off, and a quilted duck-down insulation blend for added warmth. The hem of the jacket falls at the hip and has an easy fit that's great for layering over long-sleeve tops and sweaters.

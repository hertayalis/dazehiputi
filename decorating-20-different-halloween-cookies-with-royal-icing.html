Decorating 20 different Halloween cookies with royal icing

With just six cookie cutter designs, cookie artist Grace Gaylord of The Graceful Baker YouTube channel transforms plain pastries into 13 cute Halloween cookies.

Watch as she outlines and floods the cookies with different colors and consistencies of royal icing, and then layers them with finishing details, including colorful sprinkles and an edible glitter spray.


The cookies’ classic Halloween decorations include a bat, candy, a candy bucket, candy corn, a cat, a ghost, a poison apple, a bottle of potion, a pumpkin, a skull, a spider, a spider web, and a tombstone.

Grace Gaylord also sells cookie decorating video classes for projects like the one above.


In the 2022 video below, the Graceful Baker demonstrates seven more Halloween cookie designs made on circle shapes. Cookie characters include: Frankenstein’s monster, a mummy, a skull/Jack Skellington, a jack-o-lantern, a bubbling cauldron, a spider web, and a spiral.



Find more at TheGracefulBaker.com and The Graceful Baker Shop. She’s also on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

$UICIDEBOY$ &quot;2nd Hand&quot; Video

Fresh off the announcement of some U.S & Canadian tour dates, New Orleans based duo $uicideboy$ have released a new video. While they tend to switch up their names from time to time, often dubbing themselves with fleeting monikers on a per-track basis, cousins $lick Sloth aka $crim and Ruby Da Cherry have cultivated a hell of a fanbase over the years. 

With a Bone-Thugs meets horror-movie soundtrack vibe, the duo have continued to deliver eerie bangers on a consistent basis. Their latest visual drop, "2ND Hand," is directed by Max Beck and captures their signature aesthetic perfectly. With an eerie green filter permeating the cinematography, the whole clip plays out like a fever dream of sorts, and both rappers feel right at home. Like Freddy Kreuger. 

$uicideboy$ recently released a massive collection of songs, compiled off five separate EPs titled Kill Yourself part 1-5 respectively. While the music might be a little "horrorcore" for some, $uicideboy$' influences and roots are embedded within hip-hop, and it shows within their flows and delivery. Enjoy their latest video, which was shot in Berlin, Hamburg, & Riga.

The O.C.'s Eric Balfour Marries Erin Chiamulion!

Another couple says "I Do!"

The O.C. actor Eric Balfour, 38, and his girlfriend of five years, Erin Chiamulion, 31, tied the knot Saturday in a gorgeous outdoor California ceremony. The couple exchanged vows in a rustic, intimate setting at a private estate in the Pacific Palisades. Family and close friends joined them in their celebration.

Famed event planner Mindy Weiss Instagrammed a couple of shots from the ceremony. In one photo the fashion designer bride, wearing a long-sleeve Monique Lhuillier gown, poses with her new husband, who wore a custom Zegna tuxedo. Weiss also gave fans a close-up look at the gifts the newlyweds gave to their guests. Attendees received homegrown oranges from the couple's orchard as a thank you present.

"Love this parting gift! Homegrown oranges to share with their guests. 'Orange you glad you came to our wedding' hee hee #erinandericforever," Weiss captioned the pic.
